Seeding rate

Turf-type Tall Fescue blend at 6 lbs per 1,000 sq ft. Use starter fertilizer (high phosphorus). Keep seedbed moist — light watering several times per day.

Fertilizer math

To apply 1 lb N per 1,000 sq ft: divide 100 by the first number on the bag.
Example: 10-10-10 → 100 ÷ 10 = 10 lbs of product per 1,000 sq ft.
Fall ratio target: 3-1-2 or 4-1-2 N-P-K (e.g., 12-4-8 or 16-4-8).

Mowing heights by season

Spring / Winter: 2½–3½"  ·  Summer: 3½" (never exceed 5")  ·  Fall: 2½–3"
